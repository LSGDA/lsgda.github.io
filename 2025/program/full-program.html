Presentation Instructions for Research, Shepherding, and Encore Papers:

  1. Encore Papers (Dec. 16 or 17, Morning)
    • Each presentation consists of 8 minutes talk + 2 minutes Q&A.
  2. Shepherd Papers (Dec. 18, Morning)
    • Each presentation consists of 20 minutes talk + 10 minutes Q&A.
  3. Research Papers (Dec. 18, Afternoon)
    • Each presentation consists of 12 minutes talk + 3 minutes Q&A.
  4. Poster Session for Research, Shepherding, and Encore Papers
    • All papers in these categories are encouraged to present their posters.
    • Time: Dec. 17, 17:30 - 19:00 local time (before the banquet)
    • Poster size: A0 or A1
    • Note: On-site poster printing services are not available.
  5. Please upload your presentation here: https://tinyurl.com/auadc2024
