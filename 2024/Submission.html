Call for Papers:

Submission

Relevant topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Graph data model, storage, indexing and query processing techniques
  • Graph mining techniques
  • Techniques for distributed graph analytics
  • Graph visualization techniques and system interfaces
  • Dynamic and streaming graph data analytics
  • Spatial-temporal graph analytics
  • Machine learning techniques for graphs
  • Graph analytics in various application domains such as social networks multimedia, semantic web, biological data, business processes, transport data, etc.
  • Vision papers to survey the area of graph data analytics as well as describe the future research directions

Workshop Format

    We anticipate receiving approximately 20 submissions and hosting around 40 participants. The preferred workshop duration is a full day, scheduled either before or after the main conference. The workshop will be conducted in a hybrid format, accommodating both on-site and online participants.

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission:  May 31, 2024  
  • Notification of Acceptance:  June 28, 2024  
  • Camera-ready Submission:  July 26, 2024  

Submission Review Process

    LSGDA follows the VLDB submission guidelines. To handle conflicts of interest, we will use the CMT conflict management system. All authors of a submission must declare their conflicts before the paper submission deadline. The submitted papers will be judged for quality and relevance through single-blind reviewing. Each submission will be reviewed by at least three independent reviewers.

    Please upload your submission to the LSGDA 2024 Research Track through the CMT system at: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/LSGDA2024
