Plans

Plans for Publicity

    All program committee members will advertise the workshop within their own institutes and networks. Additionally, they will promote the workshop when organizing international conferences, giving talks, and attending other formal/informal academic activities. We will also advertise the workshop through various channels, such as mailing lists (e.g., DB World) and social media (e.g., LinkedIn and Twitter pages for the workshop).

Plans for Disseminating the Results of the Workshop

    We will seek to publish accepted papers in CEUR Workshop Proceedings or Lecture Notes in Computer Science, Springer. Outstanding research papers will be recommended for journal extensions in the World Wide Web Journal.

Plans for Seeking Sponsorship

    We plan to seek sponsorship from both universities and industrial companies with the aim of raising approximately US$10K to US$20K. These funds will be utilized to partially cover the costs associated with invited speakers and other potential expenses.
