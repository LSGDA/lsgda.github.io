Contact Information

    Wenjie Zhang (wenjie.zhang@unsw.edu.au) is currently a Professor, ARC Future Fellow, Head of the Data and Knowledge Research Group, and Deputy Head of School (Research) in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of New South Wales. Her research interests include graph data processing, spatial-temporal data analysis, and uncertain data analysis. She has authored or co-authored over 200 peer-reviewed publications in top venues such as TKDE, TODS, VLDBJ, SIGMOD, VLDB, and ICDE. She received the Australian Research Council Future Fellowship (FT3) in 2021 and the Discovery Early Career Researcher Award in 2011. In 2019, she was honoured with the Chris Wallace Award for her significant contributions to large-scale graph data processing. Her research has been well-supported by 12 Australian Research Council grants and several industry-funded projects. Wenjie is an Associate Editor for IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering and The VLDB Journal.

    Lu Qin (lu.qin@uts.edu.au) is currently a Professor and ARC Future Fellow in the Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute at the University of Technology Sydney. His research interests include big graph analytics and graph query processing. His research output encompasses over 200 publications in esteemed data management venues, such as TKDE, VLDBJ, SIGMOD, VLDB, and ICDE. His work in web-scale graph analytics earned him the Best Paper Award at ICDE 2016. He serves as a program committee member for various top venues, including SIGMOD,VLDB, ICDE, KDD, and ICDM.

    Long Yuan (longyuan@njust.edu.cn) is currently a Professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanjing University of Science and Technology. His research interests include big graph analytics and graph query processing. Long Yuan has a substantial publication record in top venues such as VLDB, ICDE, The Web Conference, VLDBJ, and TKDE. He serves as a program committee member for various prestigious conferences, including VLDB, ICDE, The Web Conference, and CIKM. He received ACM SIGMOD China Rising Star Award in 2022, and Best Student Paper Award at DASFAA 2023.

    Zhengyi Yang (zhengyi.yang@unsw.edu.au) is currently an Associate Lecturer in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of New South Wales and a Visiting Database System Scientist at Enmotech Data AU. His research interests lie in developing efficient and scalable algorithms and systems for managing and processing large-scale data, especially for graph data. Zhengyi Yang has a substantial publication record in top venues such as SIGMOD, VLDB, and ICDE. He serves as a program committee member for various conferences, including CIKM, DASFAA, ADMA, and WISE. He received the Best Student Paper Award at KSEM 2020 and ADC 2022.
